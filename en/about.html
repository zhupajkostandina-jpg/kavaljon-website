About Us

Who we are and why we exist

Kavaljon Center was officially registered as a non-profit organization on June 18, 2026, by decision of the Tirana First Instance Court of General Jurisdiction (Decision No. 2100). It was founded by four residents of Kavajë, with the goal of improving the quality of life of citizens and the community.

Our scope of activity spans development, social, economic, health, environmental, educational, youth and cultural fields — always with the people of Kavajë at the center of every initiative.
