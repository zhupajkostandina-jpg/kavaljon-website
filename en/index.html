Kavaljon is the community's voice for heritage, environment, and shared life in our seaside town from the stone of the clock tower to the waves on the shore.

Focus Areas

Four pillars, like the four arches of the colonnade

Arch I

Education & Youth

Technical support for teachers, new teaching methods, and promoting an open, welcoming school for students.

Arch II

Family Empowerment

We support families in Kavajë socially, economically, medically, and culturally.

Arch III

Civic Rights

Advocacy for good governance, access to education and healthcare, and civic participation in decision-making.

Arch IV

Youth Employment

We empower young people on their path to employment and toward local advisory structures.
